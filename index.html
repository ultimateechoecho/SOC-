Game Overview

Football (also called soccer in some countries) is the most popular sport in the world. It is played between two teams of 11 players each. The main aim of the game is to score more goals than the other team by putting the ball into their net.

The modern game started in England in 1863 when the Football Association was formed and the rules were written down. Today football is controlled by FIFA and is played in almost every country.

Basic rules:

  • A match has two halves of 45 minutes each.
  • Only the goalkeeper can touch the ball with their hands.
  • You cannot stand in an offside position when the ball is passed to you.
  • The team with more goals at the end wins the match.

Game Highlights

Football has given us many unforgettable moments. The FIFA World Cup, held every four years, is the biggest tournament and billions of people watch it. Club competitions like the UEFA Champions League are also very famous.

Some of the greatest players of all time are Pelé, Diego Maradona, Lionel Messi and Cristiano Ronaldo. Messi finally won the World Cup with Argentina in 2022, which was one of the best finals ever.

How to Get Started

If you are new to football, here are some simple tips to begin:

Equipment you need:

  • A football
  • A pair of football boots
  • Shin guards and socks
  • Comfortable clothes to run in

Tips for beginners:

  • Practice basic passing and ball control every day.
  • Watch matches to learn positions and movement.
  • Play with friends to improve teamwork.
  • Always warm up before playing to avoid injuries.
